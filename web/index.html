About Us

Welcome to Farwest Tech Store..

Farwest Tech Store is not just a retail outlet; it's a catalyst for the technological advancement of Nepal. We are actively encouraging innovation and fostering research in robotics and related fields. Through our products and services, we aspire to contribute significantly to the evolution of technology in Nepal, creating a brighter future for our nation. Join us on this exciting journey towards a more technologically vibrant Nepal!.


Farwest Tech Store, founded in 2023 by Mr. Santosh Bhatta and Ram Karki, proudly stands as the premier tech store in Sudurpashchhim. Our mission is to empower students, enthusiasts, and professionals by offering a comprehensive platform for affordable access to cutting-edge robotics components, kits, and valuable resources.
